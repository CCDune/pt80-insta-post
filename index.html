This is a recent photo I personally took with my Nikon D850 Camera. My F stop was 7.1 on a 24mm focal length, 1/5th of a second. The location is a small place that you can rent as an Air BnB on Lake George in Flordia. Taking photos of sunsets is perhaps my favorite kind of photography, the lighting changes are super dramatic in such a short span of time and you never know what kind of beautiful colors you will see as the sun disapeers from view.
